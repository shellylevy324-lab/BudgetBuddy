# v0.13.3 Pilot Checklist

## Student Profile
- Confirm Response Delay defaults to 2 seconds
- Test values of 0, 2, and 3 seconds
- Save and refresh to confirm the value persists

## Universal Delay
- Trial content appears immediately
- Yes/No remain disabled during the delay
- Check My Cart remains disabled during the delay
- “Please look carefully...” appears while locked
- Controls activate after the programmed delay

## Latency
- Latency begins when controls activate
- Delay time is not counted as student response latency

## Prompt Fading
- Visual + Audio keeps buttons locked through the audio cue
- Audio-only keeps buttons locked through the audio cue
- Visual-only unlocks after the programmed delay
- Independent unlocks after the programmed delay

## Baseline and Least-to-Most
- Both procedures use the universal delay
- Other prompting and reinforcement rules remain unchanged
