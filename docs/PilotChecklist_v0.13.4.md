# v0.13.4 Pilot Checklist

## Universal Response Lock
- Set Response Delay to 2 seconds
- Begin Baseline, Least-to-Most, and Prompt-Fading sessions
- Confirm Yes/No cannot be clicked during the delay
- Confirm disabled controls are visibly dimmed
- Confirm controls activate only after the delay

## Token Prompt Fading
- Test Visual + Audio, Audio, and SD starting levels
- Confirm controls remain disabled until the spoken cue has finished
- Test Visual-only and Independent levels
- Confirm controls activate after the selected response delay

## Latency
- Confirm latency starts when controls activate, not when the trial appears
